The exact process

How it actually works.

Step by step, no surprises. This is the same process every Alabama WebWorx client goes through, from first call to going live.

01
Free · 15 minutes
We talk for 15 minutes

A short call or in-person chat. I look at your current website (or note that you don't have one) and give you an honest opinion — not a sales pitch.

What happens here

  • I review your current site (if you have one) and your business
  • I tell you honestly if a rebuild would actually help
  • You decide if you want a free preview built — zero obligation
02
Free · 2–5 business days
I build your free preview

Using your real business info, photos, and branding, I build a complete, working version of your new website. You see the actual thing — not a mockup or a sketch.

What happens here

  • I gather your logo, photos, hours, and key business details
  • I build a full working site, live on a private preview link
  • I send you that link — no payment, no commitment yet
03
Your decision
You review and approve

Look it over. Want something changed? We adjust it together, still free. Once you're happy, you approve and choose your plan.

What happens here

  • You give feedback — wording, photos, layout, anything
  • I make revisions until you're satisfied
  • You pick Starter, Growth, or Pro and subscribe — that's the only payment step
04
Go-live · same day to 48 hours
We go live on your domain

If you already have a domain, I point it to your new site with no downtime. If you don't have one yet, I register one for you.

What happens here

  • If you have a domain: I update its settings to point at your new site
  • If you don't: I help you register one (~$10-15/year, billed to you directly)
  • Your new site goes live — your old one, if any, is fully replaced
05
Ongoing
I maintain it every month

Hosting, security, and updates are handled. Need your hours changed, a new photo added, a price updated? You text or email me — I make the change.

What's included monthly

  • Hosting, uptime monitoring, and security/SSL maintenance
  • Content updates based on your plan (Starter: 1/month, Growth & Pro: unlimited)
  • One point of contact — me — for anything that comes up

Ready to see your own free preview?

No cost, no obligation. Just a real working version of what your site could look like.
