Scope of Service

Alabama WebWorx designs and builds a custom website for your business at no upfront cost. You'll see a complete, working preview before any payment is required.

Once you approve the preview and select a plan, your site goes live and your monthly subscription begins. Every plan includes hosting, domain management, SSL security, and content updates (the amount varies by plan — see Pricing for details).

Payment Terms

Subscriptions are billed monthly in advance through Stripe, a secure third-party payment processor. We never see or store your card information directly.

There is a 3-month minimum term from your go-live date. After that, service continues month-to-month and either party can cancel with 14 days' notice.

If a payment fails or is late, you'll be notified and given 10 days to resolve it before any service interruption.

Ownership

You own all content you provide — your photos, business information, branding, and your domain name — at all times, no exceptions.

Alabama WebWorx retains ownership of the underlying code, design templates, and technical systems used to build your site. If you ever cancel, we'll cooperate in good faith to transfer your domain and provide your content for use elsewhere.

Liability

We work hard to keep your site fast, secure, and online. That said, no website provider — including large companies — can guarantee 100% uptime, since hosting providers and internet infrastructure occasionally have outages outside anyone's control.

Our liability for any issue is limited to the fees you paid in the most recent month. We are not responsible for indirect losses like lost sales or missed bookings due to downtime or errors.

This is a standard limitation used across the web design industry, and it's detailed in full in the signed Services Agreement.
