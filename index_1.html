Common questions

Before you book the call

Is the preview site actually free, no catch?+
Yes. I build a working version of your new site using your real business details before you commit to anything. If you don't like it or don't want to move forward, you owe nothing.
What happens to my current domain?+
I handle the transfer or DNS pointing for you once you approve the new site, so there's no downtime and nothing technical for you to figure out.
What if I want to cancel?+
Plans run on a 3-month minimum, then month-to-month after that. If you cancel, you keep your domain — I just hand off hosting cleanly.
What kind of "updates" are included?+
Things like updated hours, new photos, menu or price changes, new staff bios, seasonal promotions — the regular small stuff that otherwise sits outdated for years.
Do you only work with certain types of businesses?+
Right now I'm focused entirely on Tuscaloosa — restaurants, contractors, salons, retail, local services. Staying local means I can meet in person, know the area, and respond fast. If your business is in or around Tuscaloosa and your current site is hurting you more than helping, it's worth a call.
